What you'll cover

Seed to harvest.
All of it.


01
Genetics and strain selection

Understanding lineage, hybrid vs. landrace, how to read a seed bank, and what to look for before you even germinate.

02
Growing media and rootzone

Soil, coco, rockwool, DWC - the tradeoffs, setup, and how your medium choice drives every decision downstream.

03
Lighting

HPS, CMH, LED - spectrum, intensity, PPFD, photoperiod, and how to dial your environment for each growth stage.

04
Nutrients and feeding

Macro/micronutrients, deficiency diagnosis, feed schedules, flushing - how to feed without burning and read what the plant is telling you.

05
IPM

Integrated pest management from prevention to intervention. Thrips, spider mites, fungus gnats, powdery mildew - identification and treatment that actually works.

06
Training techniques

LST, topping, manifolding, SCROG, lollipopping - when to use each and how to build a canopy that produces.

07
Harvest and cure

Trichome reading, flush timing, wet vs. dry trim, drying environment, curing jars - how the finish determines the quality of everything you grew.

08
Phenotype selection

How to run a pheno hunt, what you're looking for, keeper selection, and how breeders think - the part most growers skip.
